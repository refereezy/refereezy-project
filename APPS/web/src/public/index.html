Welcome to Refereezy

Refereezy is a comprehensive platform designed for football match management, providing tools for referees, teams, and competition organizers.

Our system connects mobile devices with smartwatches to record match incidents in real-time, ensuring accurate and efficient match reporting.

Main Features:

  • Mobile Apps for Referees

    Android application that allows referees to manage matches and record incidents quickly and efficiently.

  • Smartwatch Integration

    Record incidents in real-time using an Android smartwatch app, paired with your mobile device.

  • Match Reports

    Generate and access detailed match reports with all incidents, scores, and player information.

  • Team Management

    Organize teams, players, and referees from the administrative dashboard.
